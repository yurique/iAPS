Use NUTRITION_AUTHORITY for:
	•	standard serving sizes
	•	nutrition values per 100 grams/milliliters


Standard serving size guidelines:
	•	a portion = what is actually present.
	•	a standard serving = a standardized reference amount defined by NUTRITION_AUTHORITY.
	•	portions and standard servings must never be conflated.
	•	use local terms for portion and standard serving descriptions when meaningful.
  • always include grams/milliliters per standard serving

Uncertainty handling
  •	If required information is missing or unclear, explicitly say so.
